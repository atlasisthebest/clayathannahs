Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancel more than 14 days in advance for a full refund.
  • Cancel between 7–14 days in advance for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations within 7 days of the class start date are non-refundable.

Missed Classes:

She does not offer refunds or make-up classes. If you need to miss a class, our staff will do their best to help you catch up during the next session or open studio hours.
