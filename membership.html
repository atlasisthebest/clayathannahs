Benefits of the Studio Membership

Unlimited Studio Access

Access to our fully-equipped studio with natural-light and open space. Work at your own pace, day or night, with complete flexibility.

Unlimited Bisque and Glaze Firing

Your membership includes unlimited bisque and glaze firing for all your pieces. Fire as much as you create!

Professional Equipment

Work with industry-standard tools and equipment:

  • Shimpo pottery wheels
  • Complete tool library
  • Skutt electric kiln

Access to RD Pottery Tools

Members have access to professional RD Pottery Tools for precision work and advanced techniques.

Dedicated Storage Space

Every member gets personal shelf storage for works in progress, greenware, and finished pieces awaiting pickup. Keep your projects safe and organized.

Member Buddy Pass

Share the joy of pottery! Bring a friend to the studio once per month to create alongside you. It's a great way to introduce others to the craft.

Expert Instruction & Support

Our professional instructors are available during open studio hours to answer questions, offer guidance, and help troubleshoot. Members also receive discounts on all classes and workshops.

Community & Events

Join a vibrant community of makers. Enjoy free admission to member socials, exhibitions, and special events. Connect, share, and grow together.

Easy Access to Laguna Clay

Easy access to Laguna Clay (mid-range clay bodies) for purchase.

Pausable Membership

Life happens. Travel, busy seasons, or creative breaks—pause your membership anytime and resume when you're ready to create again.

Studio Member Discount for Family Classes (Including Children's Wheel-Throwing and Infant-Toddler Workshops)

Studio members receive discounts on all family classes including children's wheel-throwing classes and family workshops designed for infants and toddlers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! Members of all skill levels are welcome. If you're new to pottery, starting with one of the beginner classes to learn the basics is recommended, then membership gives you space to practice and grow.

Can I bring my own clay?

Yes! Members are welcome to bring their own clay. Purchase clay at the studio for your convenience.

How does pausing work?

Contact the studio via email or in person to pause your membership for the following month. Memberships can be paused for up to 3 months per year. Your storage space will be held during short pauses.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contract required. Membership is month-to-month. The studio requires 30 days notice to cancel.

Can I take classes as a member?

Absolutely! Members receive 15% off all classes and workshops. It's a great way to learn new techniques and deepen your skills.

Membership Subscriptions

Studio memberships are non-refundable and non-prorated. Memberships automatically renew on a recurring basis unless canceled before the next billing cycle. Members are responsible for canceling their subscription prior to the upcoming billing date to end their membership.
