Regular Community Programs

Quarterly Clay Testing

Experiment with different clay bodies and share results with the community. Make up to 10 test tiles from local clays—these will be fired and ready for your own glaze testing.

Free for members • $15 for non-members

Quarterly Glaze Testing

Experiment with new glazes, share results, and expand your knowledge of surface decoration. Glaze up to 10 test tiles or 1 kiln god.

Free for members • $20 for non-members

Quarterly Studio Grant

In an effort to make pottery more accessible to individuals experiencing financial hardship, donations to the grant and sales with 100% proceeds going toward the grant will fully sponsor one student's participation in a four-week wheel-throwing class rotation.

Community Outreach

She believes in making pottery accessible to everyone. Through our scholarship program, she offers free and discounted classes to community members who might not otherwise have access to ceramic arts education.

Community Initiatives:

  • Partnership with schools for after-school pottery programs and student internships
  • Pottery with Parkinson's program for individuals living with Parkinson's Disease
  • Community pop-ups at local farmer's markets and artist events
